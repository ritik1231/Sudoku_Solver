Sudoku Rules

  1. Each row, column and nine 3x3 subgrids must contain the numbers from 1 to 9, without repetitions.
  2. If you enter a wrong number(not a possible number for that cell ), a penalty of 10 seconds will be added to your time.
  3. Each hint you take will add a penalty of 20 seconds to your time.
  4. Every time you view the solution, a penalty of 5 minutes will be added to your time.

Note: The hint does not guarantee the actual solution. It only provides the best possible move for the current puzzle state.
